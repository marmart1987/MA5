About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a simple-to-use educational tool for people of all ages to use to improve their skills at AP and Computer. These two subjects form essential critical thinking skills that are necessary to understand our present times. True to our site's name, we try to exemplify magis in our work in this website and do our part in making a smart and sustainable future for us all.
